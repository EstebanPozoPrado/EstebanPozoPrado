Mercedes Benz Assembly line

Working on the headlight assembly stage for Mercedes S-Klasse at the gluing and stapling station.

Robot Assembly

Using robotic arms to bond headlight parts by plasma surface activation of the casing and headlight lens before final bonding and stapling.

Quality control of materials

Controlling the quality of materials before being used in the assembly line. Developed a method to repair and reduce the amount of parts with failures along with the quality control department, this led to an estimate 2000€/ day savings for one Mercedes Benz line.

Laser engraving

Using robot laser engraving procedures to comply with Canadian product standards and traceability. Worked along to determine the most common failure causes for defective final products. Which lead to a 50000€/ week saving for one Mercedes Benz line.

Computer Vision

Pattern recognition of the headlight lightpatterns as automated quality control procedure.
